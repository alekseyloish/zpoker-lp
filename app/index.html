Identity verification

To verify your first and last name, download one of the following documents:
  • Passport
  • Driver's license (both sides)

Requirements for the document:

  • Сlear color image (JPG, JPEG, GIF, PNG, PDF no more 8mb)
  • Size over 800 by 600 pixels
  • No hidden parts

Attention, we do not re-register accounts of persons under the age of 18, according to our rules of service.

Address verification

To verify your first and last name, download one of the following documents:
  • Receipt for utility payments (gas, plumbing, electricity)
  • Receipt for communication services (Internet, landline or cell phone)
  • Bank card account statement
  • Bank confirmation of an open account in your name
  • Registration page from the passport
  • Tax notice
  • Car Registration Certificate (both sides)

The document must contain the name and surname, address of residence and be no older than 3 months (exception: the stamp of registration in the passport and the address of residence indicated in the driver's license).

Requirements for the document:

  • Сlear color image (JPG, JPEG, GIF, PNG, PDF no more 8mb)
  • Size over 800 by 600 pixels
  • No hidden parts

Attention, we do not re-register accounts of persons under the age of 18, according to our rules of service.
